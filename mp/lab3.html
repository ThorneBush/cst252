Lab 3

Big Idea
Get in the habit of thinking in terms of pseudocoding before jumping into coding.

Task 1: Pseudocode a simple task or card game - with a partner!
Think about the in-class activity we did for pseudocoding where we came up with algorithms to find the highest letter in books on a bookshelf. Try to come up with a similar task or game. An example could be: alphabetizing a list of names, playing one move of tic-tac-toe or connect 4, playing chess, any kind of word puzzle, or a simple task from your favorite video game.

Describe the task or game you picked to me so I can understand it
Write down brief answers to the questions we asked:

  • What actions can I take?
  • What do I need to remember or keep track of?
  • What do I do over and over again?
  • When do I need to make decisions?
  • Write down a pseudocode process for your task.
